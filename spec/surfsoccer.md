| Section | Description |
|--------|-------------|

| **Intro** | “San Diego Surf Soccer Club” — established 1977, premier ECNL, national-team pathway; 45+ years, top levels locally/regionally/nationally. |
| **Stats** | 4 stats (e.g. National Championships, State Championships, National Team Players, College Commits) with “0” placeholders or real numbers; “21 National Championships” callout. |

| **Coaches** | Long list of coach cards: name, role (Head/Assistant, Boys/Girls), accreditation, coaching/playing experience, “Why Surf?” quote. Dozens of coaches across locations. |

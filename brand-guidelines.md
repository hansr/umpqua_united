# Umpqua United Soccer Club — Brand Guidelines

This document defines how the Umpqua United Soccer Club (UUSC) brand should be used in official materials, on the website, and in partner or external contexts. Following these guidelines keeps our identity clear and consistent.

---

## 1. Typography

### Primary typeface: Inter

We use **Inter** (Google Fonts) for all body text and headings. Inter is a neo-grotesque sans-serif that is readable on screen and in print and supports a wide range of weights.

- **Font family:** Inter, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif  
- **Source:** [Google Fonts – Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)

### Weights and usage

| Use            | Weight | Example              |
|----------------|--------|----------------------|
| Body copy      | 400    | Paragraphs, lists   |
| Emphasis       | 500–600| Links, labels       |
| Headings       | 600–700| H1–H6, section titles|
| Strong emphasis| 700–800| Hero title, CTAs    |
| Light accent   | 300    | Optional subtitles  |

### Internal guidelines

- Use only Inter for official club documents, presentations, and web content unless a template (e.g. league form) requires another font.
- Do not mix in other decorative or script fonts for core club communications.
- For printed programs or flyers, use Inter when possible; if the printer cannot use it, choose a similar sans-serif (e.g. Arial, Helvetica) and keep styling minimal.

### External guidelines

- Partners and sponsors may pair the UUSC logo with their own fonts in co-branded materials, but the club’s name and tagline should appear in Inter (or a close equivalent) when the piece is officially from UUSC.
- When providing logos or assets to vendors, include this document or a one-page summary that specifies Inter for any text that represents UUSC.

## 2. Color

### Primary palette (green theme)

| Role           | Hex       | Use |
|----------------|-----------|-----|
| Primary        | `#00b13f` | Main brand green; buttons, links, key UI |
| Primary dark   | `#009235` | Headers, dark backgrounds, hero gradients |
| Primary light  | `#00c94a` | Highlights, hover states, gradients |
| Secondary      | `#00c94a` | Success, accents, secondary buttons |

### Accent and neutrals

| Role           | Hex       | Use |
|----------------|-----------|-----|
| Accent         | `#f59e0b` | Warnings, highlights, badges |
| Text primary   | `#1f2937` | Body copy, headings on light backgrounds |
| Text secondary | `#6b7280` | Supporting text, captions |
| Text light     | `#9ca3af` | Placeholders, disabled states |
| Background     | `#ffffff` | Page background |
| Background alt | `#f9fafb` | Sections, cards |
| Border         | `#e5e7eb` | Dividers, borders |

### Semantic colors

- **Success:** `#00b13f`  
- **Error:** `#ef4444`  
- **Warning:** `#f59e0b`  
- **Info:** `#3b82f6`  

### Internal guidelines

- Use the primary green (`#00b13f`) as the main brand color in slides, one-pagers, and social graphics.
- On dark backgrounds (e.g. header, hero), use white (`#ffffff`) for text and key UI elements.
- Avoid introducing new greens or colors that conflict with the primary palette; when in doubt, use the values above.

### External guidelines

- Partners and sponsors may use the primary green and primary dark when referencing UUSC in co-branded materials, with approval from the club.
- Do not alter the primary green’s hex value in external use; provide `#00b13f` and `#009235` to designers and vendors.
- Third parties should not create new “UUSC” color variants (e.g. different greens) without written approval.

## 3. Logo

### Primary logo

- **File:** `assets/images/hero/umpqua-logo.png`  
- **Usage:** Website header, official documents, presentations, and any digital or print material that represents the club.  
- **Display:** The logo is used at a fixed height of **64px** in the site header; in other contexts, scale proportionally and keep the aspect ratio intact.

### Favicon

- **File:** `assets/images/favicon.svg` (or `favicon.png` where SVG is not supported)  
- **Usage:** Browser tabs, bookmarks, and app icons for the official website.

### Clear space and placement

- Allow clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the “U” (or the main character) in “Umpqua” on all sides.
- Do not crowd the logo with text, other logos, or graphics within that clear space.
- Prefer placement on white or light gray (`#f9fafb`); on dark backgrounds use an approved light or white version if available.

### Do not

- Stretch, skew, or change the logo’s proportions.  
- Change the logo’s colors except to an approved single-color version (e.g. white on dark).  
- Add effects (e.g. drop shadows, outlines, gradients) that alter the mark.  
- Use low-resolution or blurred versions; use the provided high-resolution assets.

### Internal guidelines

- Staff and volunteers should use only the official logo files from the club (website repo or asset folder). Do not recreate or “redraw” the logo.
- For social media, use the same logo; keep profile images consistent across platforms.
- When creating flyers or handouts, request the logo from the designated club contact or use the files in the official asset location.

### External guidelines

- Partners, sponsors, and vendors must use the logo only as provided by UUSC and in line with these guidelines.
- Co-branded materials (e.g. sponsor + UUSC) should give the UUSC logo clear space and equal or appropriate prominence as agreed in the partnership.
- Unauthorized use of the logo for commercial or misleading purposes is not permitted; contact the club for licensing or permission.

## 4. Internal use (staff & volunteers)

- **Audience:** Board, staff, coaches, volunteers, and anyone creating official UUSC materials.
- **Scope:** Website, email, social media, flyers, programs, signs, and any document that speaks for the club.
- **Rules:**
  - Use this document as the single source for font (Inter), color (primary palette above), and logo usage.
  - When in doubt, keep layouts simple and rely on primary green and white for emphasis.
  - Store and share only approved logo files; do not create alternate versions without approval.
  - Tagline: “Building champions on and off the field” — use as-is when referencing the club’s tagline.
- **Mission and vision:** Use the official mission and vision from the website or `_config.yml` for any written materials that state the club’s mission or vision.

---

## 5. External use (partners, sponsors, media, vendors)

- **Audience:** Sponsors, partners, media, vendors, and any third party that will use the UUSC name, logo, or brand elements.
- **Rules:**
  - Use only logo and brand assets provided or approved by Umpqua United Soccer Club.
  - Do not modify the logo’s colors, proportions, or composition.
  - When using the club’s name in text, pair it with Inter (or a similar sans-serif) and the primary green `#00b13f` when representing UUSC.
  - Co-branded materials should be approved by the club before publication or distribution.
  - Unauthorized commercial use of the name or logo is not permitted.
- **Requests:** For logo files, brand one-pagers, or partnership guidelines, contact: **info@umpquaunited.org**.
